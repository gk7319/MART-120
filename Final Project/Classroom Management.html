Classroom Management Tools

I have gathered several management techniques over the years of observing and teaching.

I chose two to start with and will add more later.

  • Class Dojo

    • Class Dojo is a software where a teacher can track students who are showing classroom values.

    It is also a great tool for communication between the teacher and the caretaker.

    Point System

    Each time a kid does something that represents the classroom values, the teacher can give them a point.

    Individual points add up to create a whole classroom score.

    As the teacher, you can choose what reward to give to the students that reach a certain point value.

    Communication

    As a closed communication system, Class Dojo is a safe way to communicate between care giver and teacher.

    There is a choice to create a whole group message and individual messages.

    The whole group messaging system is great for sending weekly letters out to care givers.

    It is important to communicate regularly with families

    Individual communication comes in handy when you need to contact care givers about their child between Parent Teacher Conferences.

    Rating

    I would rate this program as a classroom management tool a 7 out of 10 if used effectivly.

  • Mystery Trash

    • Mystery Trash is a tool to use when cleaning the classroom. This task is performed at the end of the day.

    Directions

    One student is chosen to be the Mystery Trash picker. They will locate a piece of trash in their mind and the other students must find that piece of trash.

    If the trash is found, the winner gets to pick out a prize from the treasure box.

    Remember: Prizes do not have to be big. Kids get excited over a hi-chew.
